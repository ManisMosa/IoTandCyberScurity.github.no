Materials and Methods

  • Arduino Uno
  • TMP36 temperature sensor
  • Breadboard and jumper wires
  • WiFi module (if not using Uno WiFi)
  • ThingSpeak account

We connect the TMP36 to an analog pin and use the analogRead() function to capture voltage data, converting it into temperature in Celsius.

float voltage = analogRead(A0) * (5.0 / 1023.0);
float temperatureC = (voltage - 0.5) * 100;

Results

Data was logged every 30 seconds and visualized through ThingSpeak's built-in charting tools.

Discussion and Conclusions

The system successfully monitored temperature in real-time. It demonstrates the power of simple IoT setups for environmental monitoring. Future improvements may include battery power, mobile app alerts, or integration with other sensors.
